8. Aftercare & Healing

8.1 Aftercare Instructions

You will receive comprehensive aftercare instructions following your tattoo. Proper aftercare is your responsibility and is essential for optimal healing and color retention.

Key aftercare requirements:

  • Keep the tattoo clean and moisturized
  • Avoid swimming, saunas, and excessive sun exposure for 2-4 weeks
  • Do not pick at scabs or peeling skin
  • Follow all specific instructions provided by your artist

8.2 Touch-Up Policy

Free touch-ups are provided within 6 months if:

  • Proper aftercare instructions were followed
  • Touch-up is needed due to normal healing variations
  • Original artist is available

Touch-ups are NOT free if:

  • Aftercare instructions were not followed
  • Damage occurred due to sun exposure, picking, or other neglect
  • More than 6 months have passed since original tattoo
  • You want design changes or additions
