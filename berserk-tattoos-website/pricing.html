📋 Important Pricing Information

  • $100 Deposit Required: Fully credited toward your final tattoo cost
  • Minimum Charge: $120 for walk-ins (covers shop minimum & setup)
  • Payment Methods: Card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), cash accepted for final balance
  • Cancellation Policy: 48-hour notice required or deposit is forfeited
  • Touch-Ups: Free within first 3 months if proper aftercare followed
  • Consultation: Always free! Discuss your design and get accurate quote

🎨 What Affects Pricing?

  • Size: Larger tattoos require more time and ink
  • Complexity: Intricate designs with fine details take longer
  • Placement: Some body areas are more challenging to tattoo
  • Color: Full color work typically takes longer than black & grey
  • Artist Experience: Our artists have 5-15 years of specialized expertise
  • Custom Design: Original artwork included in hourly rate
