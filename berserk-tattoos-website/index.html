MASTER ARTISTS

Three distinct visions. One commitment to exceptional artistry.

Amelia Kelso

Fine Line Specialist

With 8+ years mastering the delicate art of fine line tattooing, Amelia transforms ethereal concepts into precise, timeless designs. Specializing in botanical illustrations, elegant script, and minimalist compositions, she creates tattoos that enhance your natural beauty with surgical precision and artistic grace.

Ben White Raven

Realism Master

Ben's 10+ years of technical mastery bring photographic realism to skin. From emotional portrait tributes to breathtaking wildlife, every piece captures depth, dimension, and soul. His large-scale work has earned recognition across Melbourne's tattoo scene for uncompromising attention to anatomical detail and tonal perfection.

Monique Moore

Bold Blackwork

Bold. Unapologetic. Unforgettable. Monique's 7+ years pushing blackwork boundaries creates powerful visual statements. From solid coverage to intricate dotwork mandalas and dark illustrative pieces, she specializes in tattoos that command attention and challenge convention.
