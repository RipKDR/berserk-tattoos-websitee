SAFETY & HYGIENE FIRST

Your health and safety are our highest priorities. Every session meets the strictest Australian health standards.

Medical-Grade Sterilization

All equipment undergoes hospital-grade autoclave sterilization. Single-use needles disposed immediately after each session. Sterile technique maintained throughout every procedure.

Certified & Licensed

All artists hold current Victorian health certifications, bloodborne pathogen training, and comprehensive tattoo licenses. Studio inspected and approved by local health authorities.

COVID-Safe Practices

Enhanced sanitation protocols, air purification systems, and flexible scheduling for your comfort. Masks available upon request. Private consultation and tattooing rooms.

First Aid Certified

All staff maintain current first aid and CPR certifications. Emergency protocols in place. Fully stocked medical supplies and sterile wound care products on-site.
