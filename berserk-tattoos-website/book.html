BOOKING FAQ

What's the consultation process?

After submitting this form, we review your request within 24 hours. Your chosen artist evaluates your concept for feasibility, placement, and sizing. We then contact you to schedule a 30-minute in-person consultation ($50, credited toward your tattoo). During consultation, we discuss design details, create custom sketches, provide accurate pricing, and book your session.

How much will my tattoo cost?

Pricing varies by size, complexity, and artist. Small pieces (2-4") typically range $300-$800. Medium work (4-8") runs $800-$2,500. Large pieces and sleeves are quoted hourly ($200-$300/hour depending on artist). We provide exact quotes during consultation. Deposit required to book: $100-$500 depending on project scale.

How far in advance should I book?

Our artists typically book 2-8 weeks out depending on project complexity and their schedule. Large custom pieces may require 3+ months for design development and scheduling multiple sessions. We recommend booking as early as possible, especially for specific date requirements.

Do you offer Afterpay?

Yes! Afterpay is available for tattoos over $500. Pay in 4 interest-free installments. This makes larger pieces and full sleeves more accessible. Discuss payment options during your consultation.

What if I'm not sure which artist to choose?

No problem! In your design description, tell us about your concept and what draws you to certain styles. We'll recommend the best artist match for your vision. You can also call us at 0478 128 212 to discuss your ideas before booking.
