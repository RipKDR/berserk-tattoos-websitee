Tattoo Aftercare Guide

Professional instructions to ensure your new tattoo heals perfectly and looks amazing for years to come.

⚠️ Important Warning

If you experience excessive redness, swelling, pus, or fever, contact us immediately or seek medical attention. These could be signs of infection.

Healing Timeline

Days 1-3: Initial Healing

Your tattoo is an open wound. Keep it clean and protected.

  • Remove bandage after 2-4 hours
  • Wash gently with antibacterial soap
  • Pat dry with clean paper towel
  • Apply thin layer of recommended ointment
  • Wear loose, clean clothing

Days 4-14: Peeling Phase

Your tattoo will start to peel and may look dull. This is normal.

  • Continue gentle washing twice daily
  • Switch to fragrance-free lotion
  • Don't pick at peeling skin
  • Avoid tight clothing
  • Keep out of direct sunlight

Days 15-30: Settling Phase

Your tattoo is healing beneath the surface. Colors may appear muted.

  • Moisturize daily with quality lotion
  • Protect from sun with SPF 30+
  • Avoid swimming and hot tubs
  • No intense exercise or sweating
  • Colors will brighten as healing completes

Month 2+: Long-term Care

Your tattoo is fully healed. Focus on long-term protection.

  • Always use sunscreen when exposed
  • Moisturize regularly to prevent fading
  • Avoid excessive sun exposure
  • Consider touch-ups if needed
  • Enjoy your beautiful new tattoo!
