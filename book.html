Common Questions

How much will my tattoo cost?
Depends on size and complexity. Small pieces start at $150. Medium work is $400-800. Large pieces are quoted hourly ($150-200/hr) or as day rates. We'll give you an exact quote during consultation.
Can I use Afterpay?
Yes, for tattoos over $500. You'll pay 25% upfront, then the rest in 3 installments over 6 weeks. No interest if paid on time.
How far in advance should I book?
Depends on the artist and project size. Small pieces can often be done within 2-4 weeks. Large custom work may need 2-3 months. Contact us to check availability.
Do you do walk-ins?
Sometimes. Call ahead to check if anyone has availability that day. Booked appointments always take priority.
What if I need to cancel?
Give us 48hrs notice and we'll reschedule once. Less than 48hrs notice means you forfeit your deposit. Life happens though - talk to us.
